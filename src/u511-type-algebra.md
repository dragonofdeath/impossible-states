# ALGEBRAIC DATA TYPES!!!!!!!!!



## Commutativity (a + b) = (b + c);
A | B = B | A
A & B = B & A

## Associativity (a + b) + c  =  a + (b + c)
(A | B) | C = A | (B | C)
(A & B) & C = A & (B & C)

## Distributivity a × (b + c)  =  a × b  +  a × c
A & ( B | C) = A & B | A & C
A & B | A & C = A & ( B | C)
